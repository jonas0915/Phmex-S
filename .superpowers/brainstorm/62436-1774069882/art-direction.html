What visual style should the new trading floor aim for?

Click the direction that resonates most — we can mix elements later

FACETED GEOMETRY + FLAT SHADING

A. Stylized Low-Poly

Clean geometric characters with flat/cel shading. Think Monument Valley meets Bloomberg Terminal. Crisp edges, bold colors, modern and professional. Lightweight to render.

NEON WIREFRAMES + BLOOM EFFECTS

B. Cyberpunk / Neon

Dark environment with neon-lit characters, bloom/glow effects, scanline overlays. Think Tron meets trading floor. High visual impact, dramatic lighting. More GPU-intensive but stunning.

SMOOTH FORMS + PBR MATERIALS + SOFT SHADOWS

C. Clean Modern 3D

Smooth, rounded characters with PBR materials (metallic, roughness), ambient occlusion, soft shadows. Think Apple's Memoji meets Pixar. Polished and professional. Medium GPU cost.

TRANSLUCENT WIREFRAMES + PARTICLE EFFECTS

D. Holographic / Data-Viz

Semi-transparent holographic characters with data flowing through them. Particle effects, grid floors, floating data readouts. Think Iron Man's JARVIS interface. Heavy on atmosphere.
